pandemic. After Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi announced on 14 April that the lockdown in India would last until at least 3 May 2020, the BCCI
suspended the tournament indefinitely. On 2 August 2020, it was announced that the tournament would be played between 19 September and 10 November
2020 in the United Arab Emirates.[1][2][3] On 10 August 2020, the Indian government gave its permission for the tournament to take place in the UAE.[4]
The full fixtures for the tournament were confirmed on 6 September 2020.[5]
